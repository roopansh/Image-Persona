Image Persona

Upload

Upload all the images of an album at a single place and give a name to the album. After your photos are uploaded, they will be grouped in the background. Feel free to browse until you recieve an email notification of successfull grouping.

Browse

Browse through the groups made in the album and view the photos in each group. Each group has all the photos having that person.

Customize

Customise the group name to the person's name that group belongs. This will help you search through that person's photos better.

Share

Photos can be shared with you friends by sharing the sharable link with them.

Download & Delete

Download or Delete the grouped photos with just once click.

Unclassified photos dont go missing

Don't worry, photos in which we couldn't identify any person are not deleted. We store all those photos separately.

Search

Search photos a particular person or group of person with ease. You can also narrow down the results by searching for some particular activity or place the photo was taken.
